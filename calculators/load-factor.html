Load Factor Calculator

Estimate load factor from annual kWh usage and peak demand in kW. Helpful for service review and planning.

--

Result will appear here.

Formula
(Annual kWh ÷ 8,760) ÷ Peak kW × 100 = Load Factor %
Notes
  • Load factor compares average demand to peak demand across the year.
  • Higher load factor generally means more even usage over time.
  • This is a planning and estimating tool, not a billing engine.
